How Rx Medo Card Works

Rx Medo Card makes it simple and secure to manage and share your health information, such as vaccination records or test results, in a way that puts you in control. Whether digital or on paper, Rx Medo Cards are designed to be user-friendly, private, and trusted. Below, we explain the process of getting, saving, and sharing your Rx Medo Card, along with how our verification system ensures trust and security.

📄

Get Your Rx Medo Card

Your journey with Rx Medo Card starts with obtaining your card from a trusted source. Here’s how it works:

  • From Trusted Providers: Receive from pharmacies, clinics, hospitals, or health registries authorized by the RXMEDOCARD Coalition.
  • Multiple Formats: Get a paper Rx Medo Card or download a digital version.
  • Finding an Issuer: Use “Find My Issuer” on our website to locate authorized providers.
  • Visit our Support page for step-by-step guidance.
💾

Save Your Rx Medo Card

Once you have your Rx Medo Card, keeping it safe and accessible is easy:

  • Save digitally on smartphones, tablets, or link to digital wallets.
  • Keep paper copies safe and make backups.
  • Caregivers can manage Rx Medo Cards for family members.
  • Watch our tutorial on saving Rx Medo Card to digital wallets.
🔗

Share Your Rx Medo Card

Rx Medo Card empowers you to decide when and with whom to share your health information:

  • Easy Sharing via QR scan or secure apps.
  • Real-World Uses: Travel, school, workplace, events.
  • Your Choice: Sharing is always optional and under your control.
🔄

The Rx Medo Card Lifecycle

  • Issuers: Authorized organizations embed clinical data into secure QR codes.
  • Rx Medo Card: Digital or paper cards controlled by you.
  • Individuals: You store and share the Rx Medo Card securely.
  • Verifiers: Organizations validate card authenticity using Verifier App.
🔍

How Verification Works

  • Only vetted organizations can issue Rx Medo Cards (listed in Directory).
  • Tamper-proof encrypted QR codes ensure data integrity.
  • The Verifier App confirms authenticity and source legitimacy.
  • Trusted globally across industries.
📍

Where Can You Use Rx Medo Card?

  • Travel: Verification at airports and airlines.
  • Education: School/university enrollments.
  • Workplace: Compliance verification.
  • Events: Venue and event entry verifications.
*Confirm specific requirements with organizations.
🔒

Rx Medo Card and Privacy

  • Includes only essential clinical information.
  • Excludes phone number, address, SSN, or unrelated health data.
  • You control sharing & access of your Rx Medo Card.
  • Encrypted QR codes prevent tampering.

Why Choose Rx Medo Card?

Rx Medo Card offers a secure, convenient, and privacy-focused way to manage your health records. Whether you’re traveling, heading to school, or meeting workplace requirements, Rx Medo Card makes it easy to keep and share your clinical information on your terms.

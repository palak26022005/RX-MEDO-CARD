Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 25, 2025

RX MEDO CARD, an initiative of RX Medical Trust, is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect both personal and non-personal information to provide and improve our healthcare discount services.

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, address, payment info, and health-related data.
  • Non-Personal Information: Usage statistics, device info, cookies, and tracking data.
  • Third-Party Information: Data shared by partner hospitals, labs, or pharmacies to enable cashless treatment or offers.
2. How We Use Your Information
  • Process membership and provide service access.
  • Book appointments and deliver medicines.
  • Handle secure payments and billing.
  • Send health tips, updates, and offers.
  • Analyze usage to improve our platform.
  • Deliver personalized senior care in Delhi NCR.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements.
3. How We Share Your Information
  • Healthcare Partners: To provide discounts or services.
  • Service Providers: For payment or IT support under confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or for public safety.
  • With Consent: For testimonials or promotional purposes.

Note: We never sell or rent your personal data.

4. Data Security
  • Encrypted transmission of sensitive information.
  • Secure servers and controlled data access.
  • Regular security audits and reviews.

While we take strong precautions, no system is 100% secure. In the case of a breach, we will notify users promptly.

5. Your Rights
  • Access your data.
  • Correct inaccurate details.
  • Request deletion (within legal limits).
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Restrict certain data processing.

Contact us at info@rxmedocard.com or call +91 9999307517 to exercise your rights.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance your experience. You can disable them in browser settings, but some features may not work correctly.

7. Third-Party Links

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites linked from our website.

8. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18 without parental consent.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in different regions to fulfill services, always under Indian data protection laws.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect service changes or legal updates. Revisions will be posted on our website.
